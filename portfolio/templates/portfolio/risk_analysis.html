What Do These Numbers Mean?

Price Swing :
Think of this like how bumpy the ride has been for this stock's price over the past year. A higher number means more ups and downs (more bumpy).
(Technical term: Volatility)

Market Follower Score :
This compares the stock's swings to the overall Indian market (Nifty 50).
• **Score > 1:** Tends to follow the market's trend but swings even more wildly.
• **Score ≈ 1:** Tends to follow the market's trend closely.
• **Score < 1:** Tends to have smaller swings than the market or move independently.
(Technical term: Beta)
